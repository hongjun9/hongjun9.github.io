I am an Assistant Professor in Department of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science (EECS) at DGIST. I earend my Ph.D in Computer Science from Purdue University under the direction of Professor Xiangyu Zhang. My research interest lies in software and systems security in general. In particular, I am currently focusing on security and reliability of autonomous cyber-physical systems, such as an unmanned aerial vehicle (UAV, commonly known as a drone), a self-driving car. My current work deals with fundamental security issues in these CPS, including attack and defense, vulnerability discovery and elimination, and CPS forensics.

Prospective Students: I am looking for undergraduate/gradaute students working with me. There are research opportunities in the areas of Cyber-Physical Systems Security. Please email me if you are interested.
